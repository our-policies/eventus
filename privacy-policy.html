Eventus Privacy Policy


Effective from: 23/02/2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Eventus, your go-to platform for discovering, organizing, and managing events. At Eventus, we take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

To provide you with comprehensive event management services, Eventus collects the following types of information:

  • a. Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password when you create an account.
  • b. Profile Information: Optional profile picture, preferences, and interests.
  • c. Event Information: Details about events you create, attend, or show interest in, including event location, date, time, and type.
  • d. Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • e. Location Data: With your permission, we collect location data to show relevant events in your area.

3. Payment Information and Processing

Eventus uses secure third-party payment solutions to process all financial transactions:

  • a. Payment Processing: We partner with industry-leading payment processors (such as Stripe and PayPal) to handle all payment transactions securely.
  • b. Data Security: We never store your complete credit card information on our servers. All payment data is encrypted and processed directly through our payment partners' secure systems.
  • c. Transaction Information: We maintain records of your transaction history, including purchase amounts, dates, and event details, but not your full payment information.
  • d. Refund Processing: Any refunds are processed through the original payment method used for the purchase, in accordance with our refund policy.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • a. Personalize Experience: Customize event recommendations based on your interests and past activities.
  • b. Process Transactions: Handle bookings, tickets, and payments securely.
  • c. Communication: Send booking confirmations, event updates, and relevant notifications.
  • d. Improve Services: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our app's features and performance.

5. Information Sharing

We share information only when necessary:

  • a. Event Organizers: Relevant booking details with event hosts/organizers.
  • b. Service Providers: With third-party services that help us operate (payment processors, cloud services, analytics).
  • c. Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights.

6. Your Privacy Controls

You have control over your data:

  • a. Account Settings: Manage your profile and privacy preferences.
  • b. Communication Preferences: Control what notifications you receive.
  • c. Data Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • d. Data Deletion: Request account deletion and data removal.

7. Data Security

We implement robust security measures including encryption, secure socket layer technology (SSL), and regular security assessments to protect your information.

8. Children's Privacy

Eventus is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or via email.

10. Account Deletion

To delete your Eventus account:

  • Email us at: privacy@eventus.com
  • Use the account deletion option in app settings
  • Contact us through our website: eventus.com/contact
